    MY EARLY CAREER


    I started my music career at the early age of 6. I started with vocal lessons from my father and slowly taught myself how to play piano. When I turned 10 I decided that I would also learn guitar.

    Shortly after, both my sister and I's musical talent was discovered by a local producer looking to a band together by the name of "No Broccoli". I orignally started as the keyboard player for the band,

    but I was eventually made one of the lead vocalists. As a group, we were extremely successful, being flown out to multiple record labels for showcases. Unfortunately, there were quarells amongst the band

    members' parents. Before we were able to secure any deal, the band we was broken up and everyone decided to pursue their own career in music. Taking matter into my own hands, I was able to graduate college

    with a degree in physics and still pursue my musical endeavors by teaching myself the art of music production.



    MAIN GOALS AND MORE ABOUT ME

    My main goal is to make music production, whether that may comprise of me being an artist, a song-writer for other artsists, or just making beats for others. Music has been a passion of mine since I've been

    very young and I want to honor my family by continuing to pursue music, no matter how hard that may be in today's day and age. I come from a long line of musicians who also devoted their lives to music and I will do

    the same. Whether it is vocals, freestyling, beat making, sound production, vocals performance, or instrumentation. All the skill I posses are elite and will make use of them and show others my true potential.
